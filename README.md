
# Global Supply Chain Performance Dashboard

## Overview

The Global Supply Chain Performance Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to provide end-to-end visibility into supply chain operations, sales performance, inventory management, and delivery efficiency. The dashboard consolidates data from multiple sources and transforms it into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, identify operational bottlenecks, and make data-driven decisions.

## Short Description

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze supply chain performance across products, departments, and markets. The solution tracks revenue, profitability, inventory turnover, order fulfillment, and delivery performance through dynamic visualizations and drill-through analysis.

## Business Problem

Organizations often struggle with fragmented supply chain and sales data spread across multiple systems, making it difficult to:

- Monitor overall supply chain performance in real time.
- Track inventory efficiency and stock movement.
- Identify profitable products and departments.
- Measure delivery performance and customer service levels.
- Analyze market-level revenue and profitability.
- Make timely business decisions based on reliable insights.

## Project Goal

The goal of this project was to build a centralized analytics solution that:

- Provides a single source of truth for supply chain reporting.
- Improves visibility into operational and financial performance.
- Enables detailed analysis of products and departments.
- Supports data-driven decision-making through interactive dashboards.
- Helps identify opportunities to improve profitability and operational efficiency.

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Dashboard

Provides a high-level overview of overall supply chain and business performance through key KPIs, revenue trends, profitability analysis, inventory metrics, and delivery performance.

### Product Analysis

Offers detailed product-level insights, including revenue contribution, profitability analysis, order volume, and inventory performance to identify top-performing and underperforming products.

### Department Analysis

Analyzes departmental performance by tracking revenue, profit contribution, order volume, and operational efficiency to support strategic decision-making.

## Highlights

### Business Challenge

Supply chain, inventory, and sales data were distributed across multiple datasets, making it difficult for stakeholders to obtain a unified view of business performance and identify operational inefficiencies.

### Solution Delivered

Designed and developed a centralized Power BI dashboard leveraging a Star Schema data model, Power Query transformations, and DAX measures to provide comprehensive visibility into supply chain operations and financial performance.

### Business Impact

- Improved visibility into end-to-end supply chain operations.
- Enabled faster and more informed business decisions.
- Identified profitable products, departments, and markets.
- Enhanced inventory management through turnover analysis.
- Improved monitoring of delivery performance and service levels.
- Reduced manual reporting efforts with automated dashboards.
- Increased operational transparency through interactive analytics.
